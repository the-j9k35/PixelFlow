The Security of Generating QR Codes Locally

Quick Response (QR) matrix symbols are 2D bar-mappings that translate alphanumeric strings into readable visual grids. Most legacy cloud scripts track user destinations by bouncing your links through data-collection servers before compiling the image asset.

PixelFlow introduces a completely sandboxed encoding engine. By compiling your string arrays into graphic matrices right inside your local machine thread, your destination targets remain invisible to tracking nets, guaranteeing pure data isolation and instant vector generation.
