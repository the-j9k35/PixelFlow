Why Optimize Images?

Heavy images are the primary cause of slow website loading times. By compressing your image assets before deployment, you dramatically decrease page render times, optimize bandwidth usage, and improve user retention metrics across both mobile and desktop screen environments.

PixelFlow handles compression through an advanced mathematical compression matrix directly within your local browser thread. Your original pixel layout remains crisp, while unnecessary structural overhead metadata is stripped entirely.
